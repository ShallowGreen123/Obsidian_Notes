

## 1、PlatformIO 中打开 Backtrace 
错误回溯工程，便于查看出错时堆栈的调用情况
[docs](https://docs.platformio.org/en/latest/core/userguide/device/cmd_monitor.html)
~~~
[env]
monitor_filters = esp32_exception_decoder // 打开 Backtrace
~~~
